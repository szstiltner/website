Bio

Zack Stiltner is a forward thinking artist from Hilton Head Island, South Carolina. His country roots and island attitude make him easy to get along with and his music easy to listen to. He looks to convey his experiences through his writing while touching a chord with the audience. He does his best to push the envelope, blending styles of rock, pop, country, and R&B. Zack has toured the South East playing everything from college bars and honkey-tonks to fairs and rodeos, while sharing the stage with artists such as Martina McBride and Josh Thompson.
